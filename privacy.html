Privacy Policy

Version 1.0 • Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

Lamplit Path ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Assessment Responses: Your answers to personality assessment questions
  • Consent Preferences: Your choices regarding data processing
  • Journal Entries: Personal reflections you choose to save (stored locally only)

Special Category Data (GDPR Article 9)

With your explicit consent, we process data relating to your religious beliefs to provide personalized spiritual content. This includes:

  • Your Lamplit personality type
  • Devotional preferences and history
  • Spiritual growth insights

You may withdraw this consent at any time through the app settings.

Automatically Collected Information

With your consent, we may collect:

  • App usage analytics
  • Crash reports for app improvement
  • Device information for compatibility

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized devotional content based on your personality type
  • Save your progress and preferences
  • Improve our app and services
  • Respond to your inquiries

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Journal entries and consent state are stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Storage: Assessment results and preferences may be synced to secure cloud storage
  • Encryption: All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption

Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Withdraw consent
  • Export your data
  • Object to processing

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you maintain an active account. You may request deletion at any time.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
