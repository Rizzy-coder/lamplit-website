The Framework

The 12 Spiritual Personality Types

Your spiritual personality sits at the intersection of an orientation — how you relate to God — and an expression — how that relationship shows up in the world. Three orientations. Four expressions. Twelve unique types.

Devoted

The Devoted Types

Devoted types experience a deep, personal bond with God that anchors everything else. Their faith is intimate, direct, and deeply felt. They draw others closer to God through the authenticity of their own connection.

Active

The Artisan

Building with sacred purpose
Contemplative

The Sage

Seeking wisdom in stillness
Relational

The Shepherd

Leading with compassionate care
Protective

The Prophet

Speaking truth with courage
Connected

The Connected Types

Connected types experience God primarily through community and relationship. Their faith is woven into their connections with others. They understand that the body of Christ is not a metaphor but a living reality.

Active

The Server

Meeting needs through action
Contemplative

The Teacher

Sharing wisdom with others
Relational

The Companion

Walking alongside others
Protective

The Guardian

Protecting what matters most
Becoming

The Becoming Types

Becoming types are on an active journey of transformation. Their faith is dynamic, honest about where they are, and hungry for where God is taking them. They model the courage it takes to grow.

Active

The Pioneer

Contemplative

The Seeker

Relational

Protective

The Wrestler

Wrestling with doubt toward authentic faith
